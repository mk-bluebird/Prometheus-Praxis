# Prometheus-Praxis: Adaptive Implementation Engine

## Overview
Prometheus-Praxis is the Synoptic Network’s adaptive implementation engine for sovereign, neurorights-respecting execution across ecosystem restoration, healthcare, cybernetics, and smart-city infrastructure. It serves as the successor to Project: Artemis, evolving from targeting and protection into a continuous, audited planning, execution, and evolution layer for city-, body-, and planet-scale workflows under Cybercore authority.

## Lineage and Semantic Function
- **Prometheus**: Greek "forethought," the bringer of knowledge.
- **Praxis**: Greek "action, practice" (embodied, real-world execution).
- **Core Mandate**: "Forethought into practice." Prometheus-Praxis ingests high-level strategic intents and formal policies, translating them into granular, verifiable task graphs bound to specific sites, devices, time windows, and legal contracts.

## Functional Scope
Prometheus-Praxis synthesizes and extends the roles of the core Synoptic modules into one governed execution fabric:

1. **Eco-Restoration Executor**: Coordinates `Tellus-Synoptikos`, `Nova Terra`, and `Gaia-Orbis` to turn planetary simulations into daily-to-decadal restoration campaigns, scheduling drones and cyboquatic nodes while enforcing ecological corridors.
2. **Healthcare & Cybernetic Orchestrator**: Orchestrates `Hestia-Continuus` and `Leben-Struktur` to execute diagnostics and nanoswarm therapies under lifeforce and ROH ceilings, ensuring forward-only, non-downgrade evolution of host capabilities.
3. **Augmentation Execution Fabric**: Coordinates neuromorphic devices and `Aug_Fingerprint`/`BioPay` rails, ensuring firmware/OTA evolution occurs only when biome, neurobiome, and thermal corridors are green.
4. **Smart-City Workflow Engine**: Grounds Phoenix mesh workflows in executable, rights-bounded plans, routing crews and flows under seven-capital constraints and Lyapunov stability metrics.
5. **Governance & Rights Enforcement**: Reads formal rules from `Themis-Axiom` and `Pan-Ethos`, calling `Schutz-Recht` before committing any plan to block flows that reduce capabilities or violate neurorights.
6. **Data Semantics & Provenance**: Uses `Ontos-Veritas` and `Lex Machina` to ensure task graphs are semantically valid, logging all critical decisions to `Veritas-Chain` under the primary Bostrom DID.

## Core Invariants
- **Non-Actuation Guarantee**: The planning and guard layers compute risk coordinates and queue telemetry but never initiate external state mutation or hardware actuation without explicit external governance gating.
- **Monotone Evolution**: Any change to workflows, firmware, or policies must maintain or strengthen safety corridors. Proposals that relax safety or enable downgrade are rejected at the ALN/Rust level.
- **RoH Ceiling**: Global Risk-of-Hard (RoH) is strictly capped at $\le 0.30$.
- **Lyapunov Safestep**: $V(t+1) \le V(t)$ is enforced for all state transitions.
